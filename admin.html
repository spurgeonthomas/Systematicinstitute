Documentation

How To Use This CMS

A complete guide to managing every section of your portfolio site.

Quick Start — 3 Steps

  1. Click any section in the left sidebar (Hero, Value Props, Timeline, etc.)
  2. Edit the fields — all content is pre-filled from your current live site
  3. Click Save Changes — your live site updates instantly, no redeploy needed
Hero & Stats
Top of page

Controls the very top section of your portfolio — the headline, description, and four stat blocks (25+, 60+, 8, $2M+).

  • Eyebrow Text — the small gold label above the headline
  • Headline Lines 1–3 — the large headline split across three lines. Line 2 is italic gold, Line 3 is bold.
  • Description — the paragraph below the headline. Use **bold** to bold any text.
  • Stats 1–4 — each stat has a Number (e.g. 25+) and a Label (e.g. Years of BA Excellence)
Value Props
Dark band section

The three dark cards beneath the hero — "What I Bring To Every Engagement". Each has a Title and a Description.

  • The description fields support Bold, Italic, and bullet lists using the toolbar buttons
  • Click inside the description box, then use the B / I / ≡ buttons in the toolbar
Career Timeline
Career section

Your four career milestones shown as a vertical timeline.

  • Click the arrow ▼ on any entry to expand it and edit
  • Highlight Tags — comma-separated tags shown as pills (e.g. 600+ Requirements,40% Process Reduction)
  • Click Save Changes after editing any entry
Artifacts
Portfolio downloads

Your 12 downloadable BA documents. The most important field is the Download URL.

  • Step 1: Upload your PDF or XLSX to GitHub at github.com/spurgeonthomas/Systematicinstitute → Add file → Upload files
  • Step 2: Get the raw URL by clicking your file, then clicking "Raw" — copy the URL from the browser bar
  • Step 3: Paste the URL into the Download URL field for that artifact
  • Step 4: Click the Test ↗ link to confirm it opens, then click Save All
  • Raw URL format: https://raw.githubusercontent.com/spurgeonthomas/Systematicinstitute/main/YOUR-FILE.pdf
Case Studies
Selected work

Your four project case studies. Each has a card view and a full modal (popup) with detailed content.

  • Click the arrow ▼ to expand a case study
  • Card Display — Industry, Title, Impact Line, and Tags shown on the card
  • Metrics — three numbers shown at the top of the modal (e.g. 40%, $800K, 600+)
  • Modal Body — Problem, Approach, Techniques, Outcome shown in the popup detail view
  • Click Save All when done
Testimonials
Client voices

The three client quote cards. Edit the quote text, author name, and their role/company.

  • Click + Add to add a new testimonial card
  • Click Remove to delete one (minimum 1 required)
Nav & Logo
Navigation bar

Controls the top navigation bar — logo name, the italic gold highlight, the small BSA badge, and the Book a Call button URL.

  • Browser Tab Title — what appears in the browser tab and search engine results
  • After saving, the portfolio will update the next time it loads
Expertise
Skills section

Your five skill categories and their tags (BRD, BPMN 2.0, Scrum, etc.)

  • To add a skill tag — click in the tag box and type the skill name, then press Enter
  • To remove a tag — click the × next to it
  • You can also edit the category name and count label (e.g. "9 competencies")
Services
Consulting offerings

Your six consulting service cards — icon, title, description, and tags.

  • The Icon field accepts any emoji — click it and type or paste an emoji
  • Tags are comma-separated and appear as small pills on the card
Version History
Undo / restore

Every time you click Save, a snapshot of that section is stored automatically.

  • Go to Version History in the sidebar to see all past saves
  • Click Restore on any version to roll that section back to that point in time
  • The live site updates immediately when you restore
Live Preview
See changes live

Opens your live portfolio in a side panel so you can see it alongside the editor.

  • Click 👁 Live Preview in the sidebar to open/close the panel
  • After saving a section, the preview will refresh to show the updated content
  • Note: GitHub Pages can take 30–60 seconds to propagate changes
Settings
Contact & password

Update your contact links and change your admin password.

  • Email, LinkedIn, GitHub, Calendly, Resume URL — these update the links throughout the site
  • Availability Note — the line that says "Currently accepting new consulting engagements"
  • Change Password — enter a new password twice and click Update Password. Your new password is saved in this browser.
  • ⚠ If you clear your browser data, the password resets to Systematic2026!
